Data Sources

Last updated: February 2026

⚠️ Important: BlueLedger is an independent community project. We are NOT a government agency. All data below is sourced from publicly available government records and open data portals. BlueLedger does not create, modify, or endorse any data.

Indianapolis, Indiana

IMPD Open Data

Indianapolis Metropolitan Police Department use-of-force incidents, officer-involved shootings, and complaint records.

https://data.indy.gov/

New York City, New York

NYC Open Data

NYPD complaint, misconduct, and discipline records sourced from the city's public data portal.

https://data.cityofnewyork.us/

NYC Civilian Complaint Review Board (CCRB)

Data transparency initiative providing complaint and disciplinary data for NYPD officers.

https://www.nyc.gov/site/ccrb/policy/data-transparency-initiative.page

New Orleans, Louisiana

NOLA Open Data

New Orleans Police Department use-of-force and complaint records.

https://data.nola.gov/

National Sources

State POST Commissions

Peace Officer Standards and Training certification and decertification data from state commissions.

https://post.ca.gov/

Fatal Encounters Database

Comprehensive database of people killed during interactions with law enforcement since 2000.

https://fatalencounters.org/
